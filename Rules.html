CLWYD SHORT MAT BOWLS

RULES

  1. A team consists of 3 pairs.
  2. Reserves may be brought in from game to game.
  3. Games will be of 8 ends.
  4. Each pair plays each of the other 3 pairs.
  5. No practice is allowed on the chosen mats prior to the match.
  6. The AWAY team will take the first jack in all games.
  7. All play will be to Welsh SMBA Rules (2009 edition).
    Note: It is the HOME team's responsibility to organise mats and equipment.
    Measuring and application of rules may be done by a home player who MUST be familiar with the proper procedures.
    Any qualified umpire present should be called for in case of a problem.
  8. Start 1:30pm --- finish approx 4:00 - 4:30pm.
  9. For each home match, 2 mats are required (5 + 4 games)
  10. The last game should be played on Mat 1 unless time does not allow.
  11. A contribution of £8.00 per team should cover hall and mat hire.
  12. Home club should provide tea and biscuits
  13. In a true emergency, if a team is 1 player short, the opponents will draw 3 names from the remaining 4 and each, in turn, will play in the pair's three games.
  14. Results should be sent to the organiser as soon as possible after the match.
